Detailed Description

A Term holds one term of a polynomial.

Capabilities:
- Evaluate itself
- Multiply coefficient by a scalar
- Increment coefficient by a scalar
- Compare in several ways
- set and get (of course)

The term can be created, evaluated, compared, and printed. Created by Dr. Spiegel.
