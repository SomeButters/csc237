\subsection{Detailed Description}
A \hyperlink{classTerm}{Term} holds one term of a polynomial. 

Capabilities\-: \par
 -\/ Evaluate itself \par
 -\/ Multiply coefficient by a scalar \par
 -\/ Increment coefficient by a scalar \par
 -\/ Compare in several ways \par
 -\/ set and get (of course)

The term can be created, evaluated, compared, and printed. Created by Dr. Spiegel.
